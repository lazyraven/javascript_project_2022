P01
Cloud Fundamentals
2 – 4 Weeks  ·  Zero to cloud basics
▾
Understand cloud computing and AWS basics before touching services.
💡 For you: You probably already understand client-server, HTTP, and APIs. Focus on cloud-specific concepts — Regions, AZs, IAM, billing — and skip anything web-basics related.
