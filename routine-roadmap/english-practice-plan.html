English Learning Resources

A simple daily plan to build vocabulary, pronunciation, speaking confidence, listening skills, grammar, and natural conversation.

1Vocabulary Building

Vocabulary Practice Playlist

  • Learn 5–10 new words every day.
  • Create your own sentences using each new word.
  • Revise previously learned vocabulary daily.

2Pronunciation Practice

English Pronunciation Playlist · Rachel's English (YouTube)

  • Focus on stress, intonation, connected speech, and difficult sounds.
  • Record yourself and compare your pronunciation with native speakers.

3Daily Speaking Exercise

  • Choose one sentence every day.
  • Repeat it 20–30 times with different emotions.
  • Focus on pronunciation, fluency, confidence, and natural expression.
